Frequently Asked!

When should I use linear regression vs. a neural network?

Use linear regression when:

  • Your data shows roughly linear patterns
  • You need to explain your model to non-technical stakeholders
  • You have limited data

Use neural networks when:

  • Your data shows complex, non-linear patterns
  • You have plenty of training data
  • Prediction accuracy is more important than model interpretability
